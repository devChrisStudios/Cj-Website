Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BIKFAM stickers

What are the stickers made of?

All BIKFAM stickers are premium laminate — not vinyl. They're weather-resistant, UV-protected, and built to last through rain, sun, and rides.

How do I apply a decal?

Clean the surface with soap and water (or rubbing alcohol for best adhesion), peel the backing, position it carefully, and press from the center outward. For best results, apply at room temperature and avoid touching the adhesive side.

What sizes are the stickers?

Each sticker is approximately 3" × 3". Decals are designed to fit standard number plates (ODI and motocross plates).

How long does shipping take?

Orders are processed within 1–3 business days. US shipping typically takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. International shipping times vary.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — shipping is available to the US and Canada at checkout. If you're outside these regions and want to order, reach out and we'll figure something out.

Can I return or exchange stickers?

Due to the nature of the product, all sales are final. If your order arrives damaged or there's an error on our end, contact us and we'll make it right.

How does the custom 12-pack builder work?

On the Custom Pack page, click any sticker design to add it to your pack. You can add multiple copies of the same design (up to 12 total). The sidebar shows exactly what you've selected. Once you hit 12, you can add the pack to your cart.

How do custom decals work?

Design your plate decal on the Motocutz website (links are on the product pages), then upload your artwork file when you add it to your cart. We'll print and ship your custom design.

What is the Instagram handle field for?

We print your @handle on the decal! It's optional — leave the default @cjbik if you want the standard BIKFAM look, or enter your own handle to rep your name on your gear.

I have another question — how do I reach you?

Email us at Cjsbikpro@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
